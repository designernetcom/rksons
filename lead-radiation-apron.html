Lead Radiation Apron

SAFETY MATTERS


Front-only apron that does not have any lead in the rear panels, drastically reduces the weight of the apron and makes it comfortable to wear for long periods of time. It is fastened to the body with two highly adjustable, elasticated velcro straps, which cross over the back of the apron and attach to the front waist, keeping the apron securely in place and redistributing the weight from the shoulders.

Available in camo grey and black.
